Why audits become stressful — even when systems are live

Governance issues don't appear in dashboards. They surface when pressure hits:

  • ATO requests transaction-level evidence
  • Auditors ask why an expense passed policy
  • The Board challenges approval authority
  • Finance can't clearly explain past decisions

Your system may be live — but audit pressure reveals whether governance still holds.

What "audit-ready" actually means

Audit readiness is not about having data. It's about whether decisions are enforced, recorded, and explainable.

An audit-ready SAP Concur environment ensures:

  • Policy rules applied before reimbursement
  • Approval authority aligned to formal delegation
  • Exceptions visible, traceable, and defensible
  • Evidence available without manual reconstruction

Governance is designed into daily workflows — not rebuilt during audits.

What CFOs actually see

CFOs gain visibility into:

  • Policy exception trends
  • Approval overrides by role
  • Transaction-level audit trails

Every control is visible in reports ready for audit or Board review.
