Reduce FBT exposure by enforcing classification before reimbursement — not during EOFY clean-ups.

Why FBT risk quietly builds all year

FBT exposure rarely comes from one big mistake. It builds when:

  • Classification relies on judgement
  • Reviews are pushed to month-end or EOFY
  • Exceptions accumulate without visibility

By EOFY, finance teams are fixing issues that should have been prevented.

What "FBT-ready" actually means

FBT compliance only works when decisions are made at submission, not after payment.

A governance-led setup ensures:

  • FBT categorisation enforced before reimbursement
  • Policy logic applied consistently across users
  • Exceptions visible early — not discovered at year-end

Compliance is designed in, not cleaned up later.

What CFOs actually see

CFOs gain visibility into:

  • FBT category summaries by entity
  • Policy exception trends
  • EOFY-ready FBT exposure views

EOFY becomes a review — not a firefight.
