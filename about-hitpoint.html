Hitpoint is an SAP Concur Value-Added Reseller (VAR) and Certified Implementation Partner (CIP). We work with Australian and New Zealand finance teams to keep SAP Concur Solutions audit-defensible — not just technically running.

Most SAP Concur partners leave after go-live. We stay for what comes after: EOFY, ATO reviews, Board questions, and the slow drift of controls that nobody notices until it is too late.

Why CFOs work with us

  • We govern, not just configure. Other partners build workflows. We embed approval authority, exception handling, and reporting logic that a CFO can defend in front of auditors and the Board — not just explain to IT.
  • Australian compliance is built in, not bolted on. FBT classification, GST audit-readiness, and EOFY reporting are part of day-to-day workflows. No manual clean-ups in June.
  • Complex environments, simple outcomes. Multi-entity, multi-currency, cross-border. SAP S/4HANA, NetSuite, Xero. We have done this across 12 countries. We know the trick to make it work.
  • Local team, global experience. Hitpoint ANZ is led by Tony Wan and Ashley Chen, headquartered in Melbourne. Our engagement model ensures clients work directly with senior practitioners — not call centres or junior consultants — at every stage of delivery.
