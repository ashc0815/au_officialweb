IDP Education

Australia 50+ countries Education

The Challenge

IDP Education operates across more than 50 countries with 6,000+ employees globally. Expense submissions were inconsistent across regions, travel requests were tracked across multiple disconnected platforms, and supplier invoice processes were largely manual — making it difficult to gain visibility into spend or enforce consistent policies.

What We Delivered

  • Implemented SAP Concur Expense and Concur Travel to replace fragmented, manual expense processes across all regions
  • Deployed Concur Invoice integrated with SAP Ariba to standardise supplier invoice handling and chart-of-accounts interpretation across countries
  • Enabled Concur Capture for automated data transfer between purchase orders, invoices, and goods receipts
  • Established cloud-based mobile access so leadership can review and approve from anywhere

The Outcome

With a unified platform spanning Concur Expense, Concur Travel, Concur Invoice, and SAP Ariba, IDP Education achieved a single source of truth for spend data across all operating countries. The finance team now tracks the full invoice lifecycle — from submission through approval to payment — with built-in audit trails that feed directly into team KPIs. Manual processing time dropped significantly, and the business gained access to business intelligence insights that were previously impossible to surface.
