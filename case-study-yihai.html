Yihai International

11 countries Manufacturing Global HQ

The Challenge

Yihai International operates across 11 countries with complex, multi-entity tax compliance requirements. Their SAP Concur environment needed a governance framework that could scale globally while respecting local regulatory differences.

What We Delivered

  • Designed a scalable governance architecture supporting multi-country tax compliance
  • Implemented centralised controls with region-specific policy variations
  • Built audit-ready reporting structures across all operating entities
  • Established clear documentation to support board-level accountability

The Outcome

Yihai International now operates with a unified governance framework that balances global consistency with local compliance needs — reducing risk, improving visibility for leadership, and creating a foundation that scales as the business grows.
