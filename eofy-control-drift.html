Why governance drifts after go-live

Most governance failures don't happen at implementation. They happen quietly over time.

Common triggers include:

  • Business growth or restructuring
  • New entities or approval layers
  • Exceptions becoming routine

Nothing looks broken — until EOFY or audit pressure reveals the gaps.

The real EOFY risk: control drift

When governance drifts:

  • Approval accountability weakens
  • Exceptions lose clear ownership
  • CFO confidence erodes

The risk isn't missing data — it's unclear control.

What an EOFY governance review restores

A focused review helps:

  • Re-align approvals with actual delegation
  • Restore visibility over exceptions
  • Stabilise controls without disruption

This is governance maintenance — not re-implementation.
