Governance Risk Snapshot

A focused review of your Concur setup and spend controls — so you know exactly where you stand.

What It Is

A two-week health check for organisations already using SAP Concur — or evaluating whether their current T&E processes are fit for purpose. We review your configuration, workflows, and controls, then tell you plainly what's working, what isn't, and what to do about it.

What You Get

  • A review of your current Concur configuration and approval workflows
  • Identification of policy gaps, duplicate risk, and audit exposure
  • Clear recommendations: govern, optimise, or leave as-is

Who It's For

  • Finance leaders preparing for an internal or external audit
  • Organisations with Concur already in place but unsure it's configured correctly
  • Teams looking to improve visibility and tighten spend controls before a board review

This is how CFOs understand where risk already exists — before it becomes visible to auditors or the Board.
