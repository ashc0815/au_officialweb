Turn accounts payable into a controlled, visible, and auditable process

Vendor payments affect profitability just as directly as revenue — yet many AP teams still rely on fragmented, manual workflows. Concur Invoice brings structure, visibility, and governance to invoice management.

  • Gain early visibility into vendor spend and improve cost control

Concur Travel

Book smarter, travel safer, spend less — without policing your people

Business travel is one of the largest and least controlled expense categories for most organisations. Concur Travel brings structure to the booking process while giving travellers the flexibility they expect.

  • Guide employees to policy-compliant options at the point of booking
  • Access preferred supplier rates and consolidated travel inventory
  • Track itineraries in real time for duty-of-care and risk response
  • Connect travel spend directly to expense reports — eliminating manual reconciliation
